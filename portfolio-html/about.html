.about

Driven by aesthetic and function; I believe there’s a place for both, and trying to find the right balance is key. Within any form of web design, usability is paramount and can make or break the successfulness of a site. I hope to code beautiful but functional websites that can follow best practice whilst remaining unique and relevant.

Currently completing a Diploma of I.T., I hope to add the relevant skills to my wheelhouse and forge a career within development that will undoubtedly have me always feeling a little lost, striving to learn more but eventually knowing the right thing to google.

I’m a cat lover, plant mother, whiskey sour ‘connoisseur’, art possessor and potential polaroid fanatic. I’ve taken many redirections on my way here but ultimately I’ve been looking for something that can fulfil all facets of my personality and provide me with both a creative and analytical outlet.
